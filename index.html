About us

Presciense is an end-to-end provider of home automation products and services.

Presciense enables utility, telco and security companies to sell a fully packaged suite of both products and services that encompasses home safety, home security and energy management on a single, integrated platform.

Our offering is based on a SaaS model that easily integrates with a service provider's existing BSS, OSS and CRM systems and a white label solution for its products

ranging from:

  • IP cameras
  • alarm systems
  • smoke/CO/water detectors
  • motion sensors

through to:

  • intelligent thermostats
  • wireless hubs
  • smart broadband gateways
